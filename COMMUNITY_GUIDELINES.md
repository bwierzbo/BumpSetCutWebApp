## Welcome to the BumpSetCut Community

BumpSetCut is a platform for volleyball players, coaches, and enthusiasts to share their best moments, learn from each other, and celebrate the sport we love. These Community Guidelines help ensure our platform remains a positive, respectful, and supportive space for everyone.

By using BumpSetCut's social features, you agree to follow these guidelines. Violations may result in content removal, account suspension, or permanent ban.

---

## Core Principles

### 1. Be Respectful

Treat everyone with respect, regardless of skill level, experience, age, gender, race, religion, or background. The volleyball community is diverse‚Äîcelebrate that diversity.

**Do:**
- Encourage and support other players
- Offer constructive feedback when asked
- Celebrate achievements, big and small
- Respect different playing styles and perspectives

**Don't:**
- Attack, insult, or demean other users
- Use derogatory language or slurs
- Mock players for mistakes or skill level
- Engage in personal attacks or harassment

---

### 2. Keep It About Volleyball

BumpSetCut is for volleyball content‚Äîrallies, plays, training, and techniques. Keep posts relevant to the sport.

**Acceptable Content:**
- Match highlights and rallies
- Practice drills and training footage
- Technique demonstrations
- Tournament recaps
- Coaching tips and analysis
- Funny volleyball moments
- Beach and indoor volleyball content

**Unacceptable Content:**
- Spam or promotional content unrelated to volleyball
- Political or religious content
- Off-topic videos or images
- Advertising or commercial solicitation (unless authorized)

---

### 3. Play Fair

Respect intellectual property, privacy, and fair play. Don't cheat the system or exploit others.

**Do:**
- Post content you have rights to share
- Credit coaches, teammates, or videographers when appropriate
- Respect others' privacy (get permission before posting)
- Use features as intended

**Don't:**
- Post copyrighted content without permission
- Steal or repost others' highlights without credit
- Film or share people without their consent
- Use bots, fake accounts, or engagement manipulation
- Exploit bugs or abuse features

## What We Don't Tolerate

The following behaviors will result in immediate action, including content removal, account suspension, or permanent ban:

### Harassment & Bullying

- Repeated unwanted contact or messages
- Targeted attacks on individuals or groups
- Doxxing (sharing private information)
- Threats of violence or harm
- Coordinated harassment campaigns

### Hate Speech

- Slurs or derogatory language based on race, ethnicity, religion, gender, sexual orientation, disability, or other protected characteristics
- Promotion of hate groups or ideologies
- Symbols or imagery associated with hate

### Inappropriate Content

- Nudity or sexual content
- Graphic violence or gore
- Self-harm or suicide content
- Dangerous activities or stunts
- Illegal activities

### Spam & Scams

- Repeated posting of irrelevant content
- Link farming or phishing attempts
- Fake giveaways or contests
- Impersonation of others
- Misleading or deceptive content

### Minors' Safety

- Sexual content involving minors
- Solicitation or grooming behavior
- Sharing minors' personal information
- Any content that endangers children

## Reporting & Enforcement

### How to Report

If you see content that violates these guidelines:

1. **Tap the ‚ãØ menu** on the post or comment
2. **Select "Report"**
3. **Choose a report reason:**
   - Spam or misleading
   - Harassment or bullying
   - Inappropriate content
   - Impersonation
   - Violence or threats
   - Hate speech
   - Self-harm content
   - Other (provide details)
4. **Submit** - We'll review within 24 hours

**Note:** False reports or report abuse may result in action against your account.

### How to Block

If you don't want to see content from a specific user:

1. **Tap the ‚ãØ menu** on their post or profile
2. **Select "Block User"**
3. You won't see their content, and they won't see yours

### Our Review Process

When you report content:

1. **Immediate action** - Serious violations (threats, illegal content) are prioritized
2. **Human review** - Our team reviews reports within 24 hours
3. **Decision** - Content may be removed, user may be warned, suspended, or banned
4. **Appeal** - Users can appeal decisions (see below)

### Enforcement Actions

Depending on severity and history, we may:

- **Remove content** - Delete posts, comments, or highlights
- **Issue warning** - First-time or minor violations
- **Temporary suspension** - 1-30 days, depending on severity
- **Permanent ban** - Serious or repeated violations
- **Report to authorities** - Illegal content (CSAM, threats, etc.)

## Appeals & Disputes

### How to Appeal

If you believe we made a mistake:

1. **Email us** at appeals@bumpsetcut.com within **14 days**
2. **Include:**
   - Your username
   - The content or action in question
   - Why you believe the decision was incorrect
3. **Wait for review** - We'll respond within 7 business days

### Appeal Process

- Appeals are reviewed by a different moderator
- We may reinstate content or restore accounts if we made an error
- Decisions on appeals are final
- Repeated frivolous appeals may result in further action

## Best Practices for a Positive Community

### Posting Great Content

- **High-quality videos** - Well-lit, stable footage performs best
- **Engaging captions** - Describe the play, ask questions, share context
- **Appropriate tags** - Help others find your content
- **Timing** - Post during peak hours for more visibility

### Engaging Positively

- **Leave constructive comments** - "Nice dig!" beats "Could be better"
- **Answer questions** - Share your knowledge with the community
- **Support new players** - Everyone starts somewhere
- **Celebrate others** - Like and share content you enjoy

### Building Your Profile

- **Complete your profile** - Add a bio, profile picture
- **Be consistent** - Regular posting builds followers
- **Engage authentically** - Real interactions beat numbers
- **Respond to comments** - Build relationships with your audience

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I post content from tournaments or leagues?

Yes, if you filmed it or have permission. Professional league content (AVP, FIVB, etc.) may be copyrighted‚Äîcheck before posting.

### What if someone posted me without permission?

Report the post and explain the situation. We'll review and may remove it.

### Can I promote my coaching business?

Light promotion is fine (profile bio, occasional posts). Spam or excessive advertising is not allowed.

### What counts as harassment?

### Can I appeal a suspension?

Yes. Email appeals@bumpsetcut.com within 14 days with your case.

### What if I see illegal content?

Report it immediately. We'll review ASAP and report to authorities if necessary.
